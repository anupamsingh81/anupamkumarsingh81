Interests

I’m fond of Data Science and an R(cpp) enthusiast.

Current work

I am a PhD student in predictive human genetics in BCM team, Laboratoire TIMC-IMAG, Grenoble, France. My supervisors are Michael Blum (TIMC-IMAG) and Hugues Aschard (Institut Pasteur).

I am currently working on making an R package for fast and memory-efficient analysis on massive SNP arrays thanks to package bigmemory.

I am also trying to contribute to R

  • by submitting my own packages to CRAN (or just GitHub),
  • by contributing to other projects when I can,
  • by asking and answering questions on Stack Overflow,
  • by posting on R-bloggers.
